KANEKI KEN

Kaneki is the first known artificial one-eyed ghoul. His unique half ghoul state is what later inspires the idea of the Quinx. After joining Anteiku as a part-time waiter, he learns how to live as a ghoul and eventually becomes known as Eyepatch (眼帯, Gantai).

NAGACHIKA HIDEYOSHI

Hide – or Hideyoshi Nagachika, 永近 英良, Nagachika Hideyoshi – is one of the most popular characters in the Tokyo Ghoul series. He's outgoing, normal in a dark world full of mysteries, and loyal to a fault when it comes to his friend Kaneki. Hide goes out of his way to help, even when Kaneki cuts him off.

KAMISHIRO RIZE

Rize Kamishiro (in Japanese: 神代 利世, Kamishiro Rize) is a major antagonist of the dark fantasy manga and anime series Tokyo Ghoul. She is a very well known and infamous ghoul, known to having eating habits which would threaten even other ghouls in the various wards of Tokyo.
